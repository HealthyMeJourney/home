Brushing your teeth may seem simple, but doing it correctly is key to maintaining healthy gums and teeth. Follow these expert tips to keep your smile sparkling!

1. Choose the Right Toothbrush

Pick a soft-bristled toothbrush that fits comfortably in your hand and mouth. Replace it every 3 months or when the bristles start to fray.

2. Brush for Two Minutes

Divide your brushing time evenly among all parts of your mouth. Use small, circular motions to clean every surface of your teeth thoroughly.

3. Donâ€™t Forget Your Gums

Angle your brush towards the gumline to remove plaque and prevent gum disease. Be gentle to avoid irritation.

4. Use Fluoride Toothpaste

Fluoride strengthens your enamel and protects against cavities. Look for a toothpaste that carries the ADA Seal of Acceptance.

5. Rinse and Floss

Finish by rinsing your mouth and flossing to remove food particles between your teeth. This ensures complete oral hygiene.
